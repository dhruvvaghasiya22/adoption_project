Our Mission

Connecting Hearts and Paws

We believe that every animal deserves a loving, stable, and safe forever home. Our platform bridges the gap between rescue shelters and hopeful pet adopters.

Founded in 2026, PetAdopt simplifies the adoption lifecycle. By providing shelters with a portal to list animals and handle adoptions, and giving users a direct application workflow, we increase successful matches and help empty animal shelters.

Our Core Values

The principles guiding our work every day.

Compassion First

Every feature we construct is centered around animal welfare and ensuring the security and happiness of every companion.
