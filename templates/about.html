Our Mission

Connecting Hearts & Paws

We believe that every animal deserves a loving, stable, and safe forever home. Our platform bridges the gap between rescue shelters and hopeful pet adopters.

Founded in 2020, Pawdopt simplifies the adoption lifecycle. By providing shelters with a portal to list animals and handle adoptions, and giving users a direct application workflow, we increase successful matches and help empty animal shelters.

The Spark of PetAdopt

PetAdopt was established in 2026. The initial goal was basic: help regional rescue centers post digital animal listings so local animal advocates could adopt more efficiently.

2027
2027

Growing Our Pack

We partnered with 50+ shelters across multiple states. We released the official dashboard portal, letting rescue operators track applications, store health files, and host remote interviews.

2028

Nationwide Connection

Introducing advanced matching filters and pet guide libraries. We are proud to connect thousands of loving owners with their permanent animal companions every single day.
